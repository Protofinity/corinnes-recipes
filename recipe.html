Classic Lasagna

By Corinne

★★★★★ 4.8 (256 reviews)

Share:

This classic lasagna recipe is rich, satisfying, and perfect for family dinners. Layers of tender pasta, savory meat sauce, and creamy cheese make it a timeless favorite.

Prep Time:

30 mins

Cook Time:

1 hour

Total Time:

1 hour 30 mins

Servings:

8

Calories:

450 kcal

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef until fully cooked. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir the marinara sauce into the cooked beef. Let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  6. Assemble the lasagna in a 9x13 inch baking dish: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom, then layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, ending with mozzarella on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes. Remove the foil and bake an additional 15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Allow the lasagna to cool for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
